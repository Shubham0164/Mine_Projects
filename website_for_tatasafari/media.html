About Tata Safari Storme

ata Safari Storme is a credible SUV offering from Indian auto major and retails in the price range of Rs 12.36-15.34 lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). The exteriors of Tata Safari Storme are rugged and the overall turnout is intimidating. This Tata has a genuine road presence which can be easily compared to the Toyota Fortuner and Ford Endeavour. The quality of the interiors and space inside the cabin is on par with the competitors and the middle row of Safari Storme is very comfortable.
